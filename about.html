“hypha” is the filament that generates fungal spores.

they create networks that decomposes dead elements, giving rise to new living beings, generating new life and meaning for discarded textile fibers.

in a world where the fashion industry’s water footprint generates 20% of wastewater,

I believe we need to change the way we live, think, and consume.

Hifas Studio views the problem of textile waste as a unique opportunity to rethink resource management on both individual and collective levels. By embracing upcycling and handcrafted garments, we present an alternative to fast fashion. Our brand serves as a platform for learning, creating, questioning, and transforming reality, recognizing change as an essential element for adaptation.
